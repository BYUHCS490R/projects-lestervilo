What is a Pokemon?

Pokémon are fictional creatures that appear in the Pokémon media franchise. They are typically categorized by their elemental types, such as Fire, Water, Electric, etc. There are abilities and characteristics unique to each type, making them diverse and interesting to explore.

I have a passion for Pokemon and their unique characteristics! You can see the different types and their characteristics on the respective pages. I also provided a visual guide to help you identify different Pokemon types.
