My Interests - Reading Novels

Percy Jackson

Percy Jackson is a series of adventure novels written by Rick Riordan. The series follows the adventures of a young demigod named Percy Jackson as he discovers his heritage and battles mythological creatures from Greek mythology. The books are known for their fast-paced action, humor, and engaging storytelling.

Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a fantasy book series written by J.K. Rowling. The series chronicles the life and adventures of a young wizard, Harry Potter, as he attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The books explore themes of friendship, bravery, and the battle between good and evil, captivating readers of all ages around the world.

Lord of the Rings

The Lord of the Rings is a high fantasy novel written by J.R.R. Tolkien. The story follows the journey of a hobbit named Frodo Baggins as he attempts to destroy the One Ring, which holds the power to control Middle-earth. The book is known for its rich world-building, epic battles, and deep lore.
