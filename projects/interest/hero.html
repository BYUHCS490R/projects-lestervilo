My Hero - Rick Riordan

Author & Mythology

Rick Riordan is an acclaimed author whose works have transformed how young readers connect with classical mythology. His imaginative storytelling blends ancient Greek myths with modern-day adventures, creating captivating narratives that inspire millions of readers worldwide. Through his persistence and creativity, Riordan has shown me the power of combining education with entertainment.

The Lightning Thief (2005)
The novel that started it all, introducing Percy Jackson as the protagonist who discovers he is the son of Poseidon. This book established the foundation for the entire Percy Jackson series.
The Sea of Monsters (2006)
The second installment where Percy embarks on a perilous journey to find the Golden Fleece. The story expands the mythology and introduces new quests and challenges for the heroes.
The Last Olympian (2009)
The climactic fifth book of the original series where Percy must prepare for the final battle against Kronos. This novel brought the series to a powerful conclusion with significant character development and stakes.
